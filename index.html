About me

Ranjana Rajput

Fashion Designer

I truly believe that fashion designers, we have this mindset of solving problems and looking at problems with a creative eye from other perspective. So, we need to use this power to really bring solutions that are much more impactful and meaningful than worrying about our artistic ego, about the next trend for the next season. And really think about how it can change, how the whole fashion industry operates.

But today my aim is to accelerate the convergence of technology and fashion to help retailers, brands designers also solve complex design challenges while making a better future happen for people. And I am trying to see the world from what is becoming and not from what it is.
